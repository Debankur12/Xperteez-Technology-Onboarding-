Xperteez Onboarding Process

For Employees | Interns | Apprentices

Offer & Pre-Onboarding

Initial Steps

  • Offer Letter issued (digitally)
  • Candidate must confirm acceptance over email

Document Submission

Submit required documents to hr@xperteez.co.in:

  • Updated Resume
  • Govt. ID Proof (Aadhaar + PAN)
  • Educational Certificates
  • Internship/Work Experience Certificates (if any)
  • Passport-size Photo
  • Bank Details (Cancelled Cheque or Passbook Copy)

Onboarding Day (Day 1)

Initial Setup

  • Reporting time/location
  • Google Meet link (if remote)
  • Team/Manager Introduction
  • HR Briefing (Policy, Code of Conduct, Leave Structure, etc.)

Platform Access

  • Email
  • CRM
  • Google Drive
  • Job Portals

Onboarding Checklist shared

Orientation & Training

Training

  • Role-specific briefing by Reporting Manager/Mentor
  • Basic training on:
    • CRM
    • Google Workspace
    • Job Boards
  • Introduction to CTQ Parameters, Daily Reporting Process

For Interns/Apprentices

  • Task tracker
  • Weekly review structure

Active Engagement Begins

  • Work allocation starts under guidance
  • First review checkpoint within 3–5 working days
  • Continuous mentoring, reviews, and support

Exit Process

For Employees | Interns | Apprentices

1. Notice or Completion Alert

Employees:

  • Resignation to be emailed to hr@xperteez.co.in
  • Notice period as per contract

Interns/Apprentices:

  • HR will notify official end date
  • Wrap-up deliverables communicated

Exit Date Confirmation shared

2. Clearance & Handover

  • Submit final CRM updates + reports
  • Close any job posts (LinkedIn, Job Hai, etc.)
  • Return of any access/assets (if applicable)
  • All system access deactivated within 48–72 hours

3. Exit Feedback & Compliance

  • Fill optional Exit Feedback Form or write to feedback@xperteez.co.in
  • Final review by HR and reporting manager
  • NDA reminders and IP policy compliance

4. Final Documents & Payout

Interns/Apprentices receive:

  • Internship/Apprenticeship Completion Certificate
  • Letter of Recommendation (for top performers)
  • Pending incentive payouts (as per standard payout policy)

Employees receive:

  • Full & Final Settlement (within payroll cycle)
  • Experience Letter + Relieving Letter
  • Any applicable bonuses (if contractually agreed)
